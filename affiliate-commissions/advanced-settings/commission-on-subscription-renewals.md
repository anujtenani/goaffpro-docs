# Commission on Subscription Renewals

**GoAffPro** provides you with the option to give commission to affiliates on subscription renewals.

To give commission to affiliates on subscription renewals, go to the **Advanced** section in the **Settings** tab of the GoAffPro admin panel.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (269).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Settings > Advanced</p></figcaption></figure>

Here, go to the Conversion Preference Order section.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (270).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Conversion Preference Order</p></figcaption></figure>

Now, enable the subscription renewals toggle.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-01-06 1518083.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Enable the subscription renewals toggle</p></figcaption></figure>

After this, affiliates will automatically receive commissions for customer subscription renewals.
