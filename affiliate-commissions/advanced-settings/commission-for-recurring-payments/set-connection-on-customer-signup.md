# Set Connection on Customer Signup

**GoAffPro** provides you with the option to link customers to the referring affiliate on account signup.&#x20;

To link customers to the affiliate on account signup, go to the **Extras** section in the **Settings** tab of the GoAffPro admin panel.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (3888).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Settings > Extras</p></figcaption></figure>

Here, go to the Customer-Affiliate Connect section.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (3889).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Customer-Affiliate Connect</p></figcaption></figure>

Now, enable the "**Link customer to referring affiliate on account signup**" option.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2026-05-28 0204144.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Enable the "Link customer to referring affiliate on account signup" option</p></figcaption></figure>

Finally, affiliates will earn the commission on any future purchases made by the customer.
