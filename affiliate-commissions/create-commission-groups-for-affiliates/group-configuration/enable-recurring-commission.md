# Enable Recurring Commission for Group

**GoAffPro** provides you with the option to enable recurring commissions for the commission group.

To enable recurring commissions for the commission group, go to the **Groups** tab in the GoAffPro admin panel.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (176) (1).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Groups</p></figcaption></figure>

Here, click on **Edit Group**.

![Click on Edit Group](<../../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2021-08-17 005319.png>)

This will open up the Edit group section.

![Edit Group](<../../../.gitbook/assets/image (479).png>)

Here, go to the Group Configuration section.&#x20;



<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (3682).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Group Configuration</p></figcaption></figure>

Now, click on **Edit**.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-02-18 220154.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Click on Edit</p></figcaption></figure>

Next, enable the "**Automatically connect customer with affiliate**" option.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-02-18 220217.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Enable the "Automatically connect customer with affiliate" option</p></figcaption></figure>

After this, enable the "**Keep connections permanently**" option.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-02-18 220238.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Enable the "Keep connections permanently" option</p></figcaption></figure>

Finally, click on **OK**.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-02-18 220252.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Click on OK</p></figcaption></figure>

The recurring commissions will be enabled for the group.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-02-18 220308.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
