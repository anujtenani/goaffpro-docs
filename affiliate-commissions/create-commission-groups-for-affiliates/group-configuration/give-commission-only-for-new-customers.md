# Give Commission Only for New Customers in Group

**GoAffPro** provides you with the option to give commission to affiliates only for referring new customers in the commission group.&#x20;

To give commission to affiliates only for referring new customers in the commission group, go to the **Groups** tab in the GoAffPro admin panel.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (183) (1).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Groups</p></figcaption></figure>

Here, click on **Edit Group**.

![Click on Edit Group](<../../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2022-03-31 122001.png>)

This will open up the Edit group section.

![Edit Group](<../../../.gitbook/assets/image (1944).png>)

Now, go to the Group Configuration section.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (3693).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Group Configuration</p></figcaption></figure>

Next, click on **Edit** in the Other configuration option.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-02-24 174833.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Other configuration > Click on Edit</p></figcaption></figure>

Here, select the "Only new customers" option in the Give commission for new customers field.&#x20;

{% hint style="info" %}
You can also select the "All customers" option to give commission to affiliates in the group on both new and old customers.&#x20;
{% endhint %}

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-02-24 174945.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Select the "Only new customers" option</p></figcaption></figure>

After this, click on **Submit**.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-02-24 175010.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Click on Submit</p></figcaption></figure>

Finally, the affiliates of the group will earn commissions only for sales with new customers.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (3694).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
