# Assign Gift to Affiliate

**GoAffPro** provides you with the option to assign the gift template to the affiliate.

To assign the gift template to the affiliate, go to the **All Affiliates** section in the **Affiliates** tab of the GoAffPro admin panel.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (3736).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Affiliates > All Affiliates</p></figcaption></figure>

Here, click on the affiliate's name.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-05-05 154250.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Affiliates > All Affiliates</p></figcaption></figure>

This will open up their profile page.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (3737).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Affiliate profile</p></figcaption></figure>

Here, select the "**Has Gift**" option.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-05-05 154329.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Select the "Has Gift" option</p></figcaption></figure>

Now, choose the gift template.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-05-05 154404 (1).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Choose the gift template</p></figcaption></figure>

Next, click on **Submit**.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-05-05 154437.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Click on Submit</p></figcaption></figure>

### Affiliate Redemption

In the affiliate dashboard, affiliates can view the products available for redemption. To redeem these gift products, they can click on the **Redeem**.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-05-05 154606.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Affiliate Dashboard > Click on Redeem</p></figcaption></figure>

After this, they can set up their contact information and shipping address for the order. Finally, they can click on **Redeem**.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-05-05 154725f.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Set up contact information &#x26; shipping address > Click on Redeem</p></figcaption></figure>

The order will be placed for the affiliate.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (3738).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
