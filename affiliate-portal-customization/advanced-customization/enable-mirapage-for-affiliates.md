# Enable Mirapage for Affiliates

**GoAffPro** provides you with the option to enable Mirapage for affiliates.&#x20;

To enable Mirapage for affiliates, go to the **AI Tools** tab of the GoAffPro admin panel.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (3824).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>AI Tools</p></figcaption></figure>

Here, go to the mirapage.com section.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (3825).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>mirapage.com</p></figcaption></figure>

Now, enable the toggle.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-12-03 201204.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Enable the toggle</p></figcaption></figure>

Next, click on **Featured Products**.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-12-03 201244.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Click on Featured Products</p></figcaption></figure>

After this, select the products. These products will be suggested to affiliates when they create their mirapage.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-12-03 201258.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Select the products</p></figcaption></figure>

Finally, click on **Submit**.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-12-03 201307.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Click on Submit</p></figcaption></figure>

#### View Affiliate Mirapages

To view the mirapages created by your affiliates, click on **See pages created by your affiliates**.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-12-03 2013183.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Click on See pages created by your affiliates.</p></figcaption></figure>

Here, you can view the mirapages created by your affiliates.
