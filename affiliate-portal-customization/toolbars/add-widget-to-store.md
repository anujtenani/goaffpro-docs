# Add Widget to Store

### To add widgets to your store:

Go to the **Toolbars** section in the **Look and Feel** tab of the GoAffPro admin panel.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (3861).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Look and Feel > Toolbars</p></figcaption></figure>

Here, click on **See how to enable**.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2026-04-14 205215.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Click on See how to enable</p></figcaption></figure>

This will open up the Adding widgets to your store window.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (3862).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Adding widgets to your store</p></figcaption></figure>

Now, open the link to your store theme editor.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2026-04-14 205230.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Open the link to your store theme editor</p></figcaption></figure>

Next, enable the **core snippet**.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2026-04-14 205235.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Enable the core snippet</p></figcaption></figure>

After this, enable the widgets you require.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2026-04-14 20523542.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Enable the widgets</p></figcaption></figure>

Finally, click on **Save**.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2026-04-14 205240.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
