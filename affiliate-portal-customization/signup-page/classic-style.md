# Classic Style

The **Classic** style allows you to add details of your affiliate program on the signup page. This can include highlighting the benefits of your program, as well as information about commissions and payments. Furthermore, you can add or create signup fields.

To customize the Classic layout, click on **Select & Customize.**<br>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-07-29 165305.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Look and Feel > Signup Page > Classic > Click on Select and Customize</p></figcaption></figure>



Here, you can add the details of your affiliate program.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-07-29 165346.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Add the details of your program</p></figcaption></figure>

After this, click on **Save**

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-07-29 1654463.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Click on Save</p></figcaption></figure>

After saving, the signup page will refresh, and the information added will be displayed on the right side of the page.
