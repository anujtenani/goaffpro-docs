# Modern Style

The **Modern** style allows you to add a background image on the signup page. You can overlay instructions on the image, highlight features of your affiliate program, and create or edit signup fields.

To customize the Modern layout, click on **Select & Customize.**

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-07-28 195821.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Look and Feel > Signup Page > Modern > Click on Select and Customize</p></figcaption></figure>

### Background Image

Here you can choose an image from the gallery, search for the type of image you need, and select it from the available options. You also have the option to upload your own image.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-07-28 195929.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Select the background image</p></figcaption></figure>

### Overlay Content

Next, you can add information about your affiliate program in the overlay content box.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-07-28 200104.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Add information in the overlay content box</p></figcaption></figure>

### Features

You can highlight the features of your affiliate program in the features field. This field can be used to emphasize the benefits of your affiliate program.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-07-28 200152.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Highlight features</p></figcaption></figure>

### Signup Button Text

You also have the option to edit the text on the signup button.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-07-28 200240.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Edit the signup button text</p></figcaption></figure>

Finally, click on **Save**.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-07-28 2005243.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Click on Save</p></figcaption></figure>

After saving, the signup page refreshes. The background image is displayed on the left side, showing information, while the signup fields are located on the right side. The highlighted features are presented above the signup fields.
