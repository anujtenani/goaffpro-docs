# View Referral Details

To view referral details such as the order date, the products ordered, the order amount, and more, log into your affiliate account on the login page.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (3541).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Login Page</p></figcaption></figure>

After logging into your affiliate account, the Home tab opens. In the **summary table**, you can view details about your referrals, such as:

* **Referrals**: unique visits made using the affiliate's referral link.&#x20;
* **Orders**: number of orders referred by the affiliate.&#x20;
* **Conversions**: orders/referrals \* 100&#x20;
* **Sales**: the sales revenue amount referred by the affiliate.&#x20;
* **Earnings**: the commission earned by the affiliate.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-04-21 1007342.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Summary</p></figcaption></figure>

Additionally, you can also view the order details table. It can display information such as order date, products purchased, order amount, customer information, etc.

{% hint style="info" %}
The order details table will only be displayed if the merchant has [enabled](../../affiliate-portal-customization/affiliate-dashboard/display-order-details-to-affiliates/) it in their program.&#x20;
{% endhint %}
