# Move Digital Assets to Folders

**GoAffPro** provides you with the option to move digital assets to folders.&#x20;

{% hint style="info" %}
You can use this feature to organize various types of digital assets by creating folders for images, videos, documents, and more. You can then move the uploaded digital assets into these folders.
{% endhint %}

To move digital assets to folders, go to the **Digital Assets** tab in the GoAffPro admin panel.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (3527).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Digital Assets</p></figcaption></figure>

#### Create the new folder

Here, click on **New folder**.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-05-27 193526.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Click on New folder</p></figcaption></figure>

Now, set the folder name.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-05-27 193539.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Set the folder name</p></figcaption></figure>

Next, click on **Create**.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-05-27 193617.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Click on Create</p></figcaption></figure>

After this, go back to the home section.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-05-27 193640.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Go back to the home section</p></figcaption></figure>

#### Move digital assets to the folder

Here, click on **Bulk Actions**.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-05-27 193654.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Click on Bulk Actions</p></figcaption></figure>

Now, select the file.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-05-27 193707.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Select the file</p></figcaption></figure>

Next, click on the **move selected files** icon.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-05-27 193744.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Click on the move selected files icon</p></figcaption></figure>

After this, select the folder.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-05-27 193802.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Select the folder</p></figcaption></figure>

Finally, click on **Move**.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-05-27 193816.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Click on Move</p></figcaption></figure>

The digital asset will get moved to the folder.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (3528).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
