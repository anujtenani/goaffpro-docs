# Reports

In the **Reports** tab, you can download CSV data reports of your affiliate marketing data.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (3782).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Here, you can download the data for stores, orders/sales, payout history, and traffic. You can filter this data by time period and store.
