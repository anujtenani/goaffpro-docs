# Settings

In the **Settings** tab, you can set up your profile information, mailing address, social profile, and account information.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (123).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Settings</p></figcaption></figure>

### Profile

In the **Profile** section, you can set your profile picture, name, default referral code, and a short bio. You also have the option to make your profile public, allowing brands to reach you.&#x20;

{% hint style="info" %}
The **referral code** is the unique code present at the end of your referral link.&#x20;
{% endhint %}

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (3784).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Profile</p></figcaption></figure>

### Address

In the **Address** section, you can set up your mailing address information like address line, city, state, country, and postal code.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (125).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Address</p></figcaption></figure>

### Social Profile

In the **Social Profile** section, you can provide the link to your social media account or your website/page URL where you will be promoting merchant products.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (126).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Social Profile</p></figcaption></figure>

### System Settings

In the **System Settings** section, you have the option to change your account email address and password.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (127).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>System Settings</p></figcaption></figure>
