# Payouts

In the **Payouts** tab, you can view the commission payments made to you by store merchants.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (3777).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Payouts</p></figcaption></figure>

### Pending payments

In the **Pending Payments** section, you can view all commission payments that are still outstanding. Here, you can see the store name and the amount of pending commission.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (3779).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Pending payments</p></figcaption></figure>

### Payment History

In the **Payment History** section, you can view the payments made to you by the store merchant. Here, you can see the store name, payment date, details, and amount.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (3781).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Payment History</p></figcaption></figure>
