# AI Tools

The **AI Tools** tab provides merchants with a robust suite of AI tools aimed at helping them and their influencers enhance their online presence and optimize revenue potential.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (3815).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>AI Tools</p></figcaption></figure>

### Mirapage.com

Mirapage provides an AI-enhanced "Shop the Look" page builder for your affiliates.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (3816).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Mirapage.com</p></figcaption></figure>
