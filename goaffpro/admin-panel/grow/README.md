### AI Tools

Offers a powerful suite of **AI tools** designed to help you and your influencers grow their online presence and maximize revenue potential.

### Recruit

It lets you search and invite influencers to your affiliate program. You can search for influencers, check their details, and invite them.&#x20;

{% content-ref url="recruit.md" %}
[recruit.md](recruit.md)
{% endcontent-ref %}

### Marketplace

It allows you to list your store in the GoAffPro **marketplace directory** from which affiliates can enroll in your program. It enables you to reach a larger number of affiliates.

{% content-ref url="marketplace.md" %}
[marketplace.md](marketplace.md)
{% endcontent-ref %}

### **Post Checkout Popup:**

Once customers complete the checkout process, a popup will appear on the order confirmation page inviting them to join your affiliate program. You can choose from three different popup styles, each with unique features.

{% content-ref url="post-checkout-popup.md" %}
[post-checkout-popup.md](post-checkout-popup.md)
{% endcontent-ref %}

### Marketing

Create **email marketing campaigns** for affiliates. You can set up the email template, select the email audience, and schedule the emails for affiliates.

{% content-ref url="marketing.md" %}
[marketing.md](marketing.md)
{% endcontent-ref %}

### **Multi-Level**

* Affiliates can recruit their **downline** affiliates, allowing them to earn commission on sales made by those downlines.
* You can configure the **levels and commission** structure for the multi-level affiliate network.
* Also, you can choose whether to display the sponsor on the affiliate signup page and determine the method for calculating level commissions.

{% content-ref url="multi-level.md" %}
[multi-level.md](multi-level.md)
{% endcontent-ref %}

### **Analytics**

You can view graphs and statistics that track visits through affiliate links, showing orders, revenue, and unique visits. The visits table offers details like date, page, affiliate, page views, and orders.

{% content-ref url="analytics.md" %}
[analytics.md](analytics.md)
{% endcontent-ref %}
