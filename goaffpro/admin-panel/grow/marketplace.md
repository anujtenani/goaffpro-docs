# Marketplace

The **Marketplace** is a curated directory of merchants utilizing GoAffPro. The marketplace tab allows you to set up your store's listing so that it can be discovered by affiliates registered within the GoAffPro affiliate network.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (3710).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Marketplace</p></figcaption></figure>

#### Icons and Logo

The **Icons and Logo** option can be used to upload an icon and logo of your store, this will be displayed in the marketplace directory.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (3711).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Icons and Logo</p></figcaption></figure>

#### Listing Details

The **Listing Details** option allows you to provide important information about your store and business. You can also include the benefits in the description that an affiliate will get if they register for your program.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (3712).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Listing Details</p></figcaption></figure>

#### Geo Restriction

In the **Geo Restriction** option, you can restrict affiliates from applying to your store based on the countries you select.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (3713).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Geo Restriction</p></figcaption></figure>

{% hint style="info" %}
To allow affiliates from all countries, you can leave the field empty.&#x20;
{% endhint %}
