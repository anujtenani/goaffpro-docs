#### Assign Coupons to Affiliates

The process for [assigning coupons](https://docs.goaffpro.com/affiliate-coupons/assign-coupon-to-affiliate) to affiliates has been updated for improved clarity. You now have clearer options for assigning coupons, whether you want to create a new coupon code in your store or use an existing store coupon to assign to an affiliate.

You can assign coupons to affiliates from the Coupons > Coupon Based Commissions > Assign coupon to affiliate.&#x20;
