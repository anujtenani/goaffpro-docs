# Affiliate Store

#### Here we take a look at how the affiliate store works:

To see how the affiliate store works, go to the **Store** tab in the affiliate's dashboard.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (50).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Affiliate Dashboard > Store</p></figcaption></figure>

Here, affiliates can search for the products in your store.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-12-25 011524.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Search for products</p></figcaption></figure>

To add a product to the cart, click on **Add to cart**.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-12-25 0115243.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Click on Add to cart</p></figcaption></figure>

Now, click on the cart icon to open it.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-12-25 011528.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Click on the cart icon</p></figcaption></figure>

This will open up the cart.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (51).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Cart window</p></figcaption></figure>

After this, click on **Checkout**.&#x20;

{% hint style="info" %}
Affiliates have the option to share the cart link or the checkout link. They also have the option to save the cart.&#x20;
{% endhint %}

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-12-25 011532.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Click on Checkout</p></figcaption></figure>
