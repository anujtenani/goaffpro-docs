# Transfer Account Ownership

**GoAffPro** provides you with the option to transfer the program ownership to another account.&#x20;

To transfer ownership to another account, go to the **Extras** section of the **Settings** tab in the GoAffPro admin panel.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (2).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Settings > Extras</p></figcaption></figure>

Here, go to the Account owner section.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (3857).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Account owner</p></figcaption></figure>

Now, click on **Transfer program ownership**.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2026-04-01 223300.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Click on Transfer program ownership</p></figcaption></figure>

This will open up the Transfer account ownership window.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (3858).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Transfer account ownership</p></figcaption></figure>

Next, set the account email address.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2026-04-01 223306.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Set the account email address</p></figcaption></figure>

After this, click on **Submit**.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2026-04-01 223330.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Click on Submit</p></figcaption></figure>

The account email will be updated.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2026-04-01 2233342.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
