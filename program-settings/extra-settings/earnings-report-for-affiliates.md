# Earnings Report for Affiliates

**GoAffPro** provides you with the option to enable affiliates to download their payout report.

To enable affiliates to download their payout report, go to the **Extras** section in the Settings tab of the GoAffPro admin panel.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (69).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Settings > Extras</p></figcaption></figure>

Here, go to the Earnings Reports section.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (70).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Earnings Reports</p></figcaption></figure>

Now, enable the toggle.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-11-05 143928.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Enable the toggle</p></figcaption></figure>

You also have the option to customize the report template by clicking on the **Customize Template**.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (71).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Customize Report Template</p></figcaption></figure>

#### Downloading the Earnings Report

Affiliates can download the payout report from the Payments tab in their dashboard by clicking on the report icon.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-11-05 160611.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Affiliate Dashboard > Payments > Click on the Report icon</p></figcaption></figure>

Next, they can choose the "Download as PDF" option to obtain the payout report.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-11-05 1610274.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Download as PDF</p></figcaption></figure>

The default payout report will look like this.
